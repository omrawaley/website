Welcome to my site!

I needed someplace to store my thoughts, writing, and opinions. I love to write about different topics, talk about solving problems, and discuss different issues, so what better place than a blog?

As a programmer, personal websites are simply invaluable growth resources. I very frequently end up at personal websites when researching different niche topics or looking for solutions.

Some shoutouts:

They're amazing because you get to learn about the topic or technique through people's experiences, not from a textbook. You just can't beat personal websites when it comes to self-directed learning.

I've been wanting to make this for a really long time, but it just wasn't a priority as my hands were full building <a href="https://pocketbyte.co">Pocketbyte</a>.

This week, I've finally gotten around to it, which you're looking at right now!

I have a lot of Obsidian notes on electronics, computer architecture, software development, design, business, and much more that I'd like to share on this site. Plus, I'd like to post about my own experiences and insights building consumer electronics, pitching businesses, and designing software.
